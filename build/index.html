Welcome

Welcome to your P.A.M device.

To begin, click on the dropdown menu in the top left and select Connect Bluetooth.

Ensure both bluetooth and location are enabled, and select your P.A.M device from the menu.

Please be patient while your P.A.M device transfers your daily data to this application.

Once the loading wheel has disappeared, you will be able to view any recorded data by selecting it in the dropdown menu.

If for some reason the connection fails, please try again, or refresh the application.

If you have any further questions or issues, contact us here.
